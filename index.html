Neighbors lifting neighbors.

A Connecticut 501(c)(3) community foundation — microgrants, mutual aid, and a yearly Easter basket drive.

Mission

Show up for our neighbors.

The strongest safety net is a neighbor who shows up. We exist to remove friction between people who want to help and the families who need help most.

What We Do

Three programs.

Community Microgrants

Small, fast grants for immediate, tangible needs. Decisions in weeks.

Neighbor-to-Neighbor Support

Meal trains, ride-sharing, school-supply drives — coordinated by volunteers.

Easter Basket Drive

Every spring, baskets for children across southeastern Connecticut.
